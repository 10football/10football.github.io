WELCOME TO THE TEN FOOTBALL MANAGEMENT: DEDICATION, PROFESSIONALISM, TRANSPARENCY, LOYALTY.

Founded in 2016, Ten Football Management specialises in the career management of professional footballers, including World Champions as well as young and amateur players.

With a highly developed network at the international level, the agency offers personalised support to each player through a unique "know-how".

Not only a sporting partner, Ten Football Management also provides advice on all aspects of everyday and family life, with one goal in mind: the players' well-being.

Beyond the sporting aspects,Ten Football Management Football Management is dedicated to assisting its athletes off the field. Its experts and collaborators assist you in many areas

LIFESTYLE QUALITY

A team of specialists (osteopath, physical trainer) is on hand to adapt programmes to fit all needs.

SCOUTING

The agency has a global recruiting team that identifies young talents and high-potential athletes.

VIDEO ANALYSIS

Ten Football Management provides each of its players with post-match reports, as well as on-demand footage to further examine their performances through a video cell dedicated to the agency

CONCIERGE

Ten Football Management provides a variety of services to its players to make their lives easier (relocation, travel planning and administrative support among others.)

LEGAL ADVICE

Ten Football Management collaborates with a sports lawyer/attorney who specialises in both sports and business law. The purpose is to help and advise the player based on his specific needs

WEALTH MANAGEMENT

Ten Football Management provides investment and financial advice to its athletes through a partnership with an expert.

SCHOOL MONITORING

Ten Football Management s dedicated to assisting its younger players in their studies at school or university, in order to better prepare them for post-career management.

PUBLIC RELATION & SPONSORS

The Agency is in charge of managing and negotiating contracts between its athletes and the world's most well-known branding companies in sports and other fields.

COMMUNICATION, MEDIA & SOCIAL MEDIA

Players can receive media training from Ten Football Management to help them prepare for press conferences. In addition, the agency is dedicated to strengthening the image of its athletes on social media through a customised plan
